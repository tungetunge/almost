Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond to all messages within 2 working days. Phone calls are usually returned the same day if received during office hours.

Is your service confidential?

Yes, all conversations and correspondence are kept confidential in accordance with data protection laws.

Do you provide medical advice?

We provide information and support but cannot give medical advice. We always encourage you to speak with your GP or specialist.

Can family members contact you?

Absolutely! We support family members, carers, and friends of people with NPH.
