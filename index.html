Normal Pressure Hydrocephalus (NPH)

Early help changes lives

If you or someone you know has walking difficulties, bladder problems, and memory issues, it could be NPH – a treatable condition.

Understanding NPH

👥

Walking Problems

Difficulty walking, shuffling gait, feeling unsteady, or frequent falls may be signs of NPH.

Learn more →
💙

Bladder Problems

Urgency, frequency, or loss of bladder control can be symptoms of NPH, often the first to appear.

Learn more →
📖

Memory Changes

Mild memory problems, confusion, or changes in thinking may develop alongside other NPH symptoms.

Learn more →

Don't Wait - Get Help Today

NPH can be treated successfully when caught early. If you recognize these symptoms, speak to your GP or contact us.
