Why Your Support Matters

NPH affects thousands of people in the UK, but awareness is still low among both the public and healthcare professionals.

  • Many cases go undiagnosed for years
  • Families struggle to find reliable information
  • GPs need better resources to recognize NPH
  • Treatment can dramatically improve quality of life
